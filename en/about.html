About Us

We're fans who travel. And travelers who are fans. We built the platform we wish we'd had before our first away-day.

Our Mission

No fan should travel blind

HinchaViajero was born from a real frustration: every time we wanted to travel to watch our team, the information was scattered, outdated, or simply didn't exist in Spanish. Travel guides talked about European trains. Forums were full of stale data. Safety advice was generic. So we decided to build what was missing: a platform that combines a fan's passion with the real logistics of traveling across Latin America. Every guide is written by someone who's already made that trip, already crossed that border, already eaten at the choripan stand outside the stadium.

What Drives Us

Three principles that guide everything we do.

Real Information

We don't copy guides from other sites or translate European content. Every data point comes from firsthand experience or from traveling fans we've verified. If we haven't lived it or validated it, we don't publish it.

Community First

HinchaViajero belongs to the fan community. The testimonials, corrections, and new routes come from real fans who share their experiences so others can travel better.

Safety Without Fear

We talk about safety honestly, without alarming or downplaying. We tell you exactly what to expect in every city, every stadium, and every route so you travel with information, not fear.
